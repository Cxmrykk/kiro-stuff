# DeskMate – Sample Q&A Exchanges

These demonstrate the bot's capabilities for the hackathon pitch.

---

## Sample 1: Direct FAQ Match

**User:** What's the Wi-Fi password?

**DeskMate:** The office Wi-Fi network is **AcmeCorp-Staff**. The password is `Welcome2024!`. For guest access, use network **AcmeCorp-Guest** with password `Guest@Acme`. Both reset monthly on the 1st.

> 📄 *IT Handbook, Section 2.1 – Network Access*

---

## Sample 2: Natural Language Paraphrase

**User:** How many days off do I get per year?

**DeskMate:** You receive **20 days annual leave** plus public holidays. Sick leave is **10 days/year** (no doctor's note needed for ≤3 consecutive days). Request time off through **BambooHR** at least 2 weeks in advance. Unused leave carries over up to 5 days into the next year.

> 📄 *Employee Handbook, Section 5.1 – Leave Entitlements*

---

## Sample 3: Multi-Step / Expense Process

**User:** I bought lunch for a client meeting. How do I get reimbursed?

**DeskMate:** Submit expenses through **Expensify** (app or web). Take a photo of your receipt, categorize the expense, and submit within 30 days of purchase. Reports under $100 are auto-approved. Over $100 requires manager approval. Reimbursements process within 5 business days.

> 📄 *Finance Policy, Section 4.2 – Expense Reimbursement*

---

## Sample 4: Graceful Fail (Cannot Answer)

**User:** What's the policy on bringing a skateboard to the office?

**DeskMate:** Hmm, I don't have a specific answer for that one. 🤔

Here's what I'd suggest:
- **Ask your buddy** – they know the ropes
- **Contact HR** – hr@acmecorp.com (ext. 4100)
- **Check the intranet** – intranet.acmecorp.com

Or try rephrasing your question — I might know it by a different name!

> *No source — escalated to human contact*
