## Section 1 – Employment

### 1.1 Employment Terms
Your employment contract is stored in BambooHR under 'My Documents'. It outlines your role, salary, benefits, notice period, and other key terms. Contact HR (hr@acmecorp.com) with any questions.

### 1.2 Probation
All new employees serve a 3-month probationary period. Check-ins occur at weeks 4, 8, and 12 with your manager. Successful completion is confirmed in writing. During probation, the notice period is 1 week.

### 1.3 Notice Period
After probation, the notice period is 4 weeks for both employee and employer. Resignation should be submitted in writing to your manager and HR. An exit interview is conducted in your final week.

## Section 2 – Workplace

### 2.1 Office Location
AcmeCorp HQ is at 123 Innovation Drive, Level 4–6, Tech District, Melbourne VIC 3000. Nearest transport: Central Station (5-min walk). Bus routes 109 and 96 stop outside.

### 2.2 Building Access
Your access badge works at all entry points (front door, elevator, garage). Building hours: 7 AM – 9 PM weekdays. After-hours access requires Facilities approval. Report lost badges to Security (ext. 4500) immediately.

### 2.3 Dress Code
Smart casual is the standard. Jeans are fine; no flip-flops or gym wear. Fridays are fully casual. Business casual expected for client meetings.

## Section 3 – Working Arrangements

### 3.1 Working Hours
Core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM. Flex your start between 7:30–10:00 and finish between 4:00–6:30. Complete 8 hours per day. Lunch is flexible within core hours.

### 3.2 Remote Work Policy
Hybrid model: 3 days in-office (Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday), 2 days remote (Monday/Friday). Additional remote days require manager approval. Be available on Slack during core hours when remote.

### 3.3 Overtime
Salaried employees may take time in lieu for significant extra hours (pre-approved). Hourly workers paid 1.5x for overtime. Weekend work requires manager approval.

---

## Section 4 – Finance

### 4.1 Payroll Schedule
Salary paid on the 25th of each month via direct deposit. If the 25th falls on a weekend, payment is the preceding Friday. Payslips available in BambooHR under 'My Pay'.

### 4.2 Expense Reimbursement
Submit expenses through Expensify (app or web). Photograph receipts, categorize expenses, and submit within 30 days. Reports under $100 are auto-approved; over $100 requires manager sign-off. Reimbursements process within 5 business days.

### 4.3 Business Travel
Book through TravelPerk (SSO). Get manager approval before booking. Policy: economy flights, 4-star hotels maximum, $50/day meal allowance. Book 2+ weeks ahead for best rates.

### 4.4 Equity Program
Full-time employees receive stock options after 12 months. Vesting: 4-year schedule with 1-year cliff. Details in your offer letter. Questions: finance@acmecorp.com.

---

## Section 5 – Leave

### 5.1 Annual Leave
20 days annual leave plus public holidays. Request via BambooHR at least 2 weeks in advance. Up to 5 unused days carry over to the next year.

### 5.2 Sick Leave
10 days per year. No doctor's note required for 3 or fewer consecutive days. Medical certificate required for 4+ days. Notify your manager before your start time.

### 5.3 Parental Leave
Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid. Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks fully paid. Applies to birth, adoption, and surrogacy. Can be taken flexibly within the first 12 months. Notify HR 8+ weeks before planned start.

## Section 9 – Compliance

### 9.1 Anti-Harassment Policy
Zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or misconduct. Report to: manager, HR (hr@acmecorp.com), anonymous hotline (1-800-555-SAFE), or Ethics Portal on intranet. All reports confidential. No retaliation.

### 9.2 Confidentiality & IP
NDA signed as part of contract. Do not share company information externally. Do not discuss unreleased projects on social media. IP created during employment belongs to the company. Report suspected breaches to IT.

### 9.3 Social Media Policy
You may share that you work here and positive general experiences. Do not share: unreleased products, internal metrics, confidential info, client details. Add disclaimer that views are your own.
